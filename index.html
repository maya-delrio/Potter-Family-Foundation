Who We Are

The Potter Family Foundation stands as a beacon of hope for underserved populations. We believe that by strengthening the family unit and providing access to fundamental resources, we can spark a generational shift toward prosperity.

As a registered 501(c)(3) organization, our operations are built on a foundation of radical transparency, integrity, and a deep-seated love for our neighbors.

Our Vision

To create a world where every family has the health, education, and stability required to reach their highest potential.

Our Core Initiatives

Educational Equity

Providing the next generation with scholarships, modern learning tools, and mentorship to bridge the achievement gap in our schools.

Community Health

Our health initiatives focus on preventive care, mental health advocacy, and bringing mobile clinics to neighborhoods with limited access.

Family Stabilization

Through direct emergency grants, we assist families in crisis to ensure no child goes without a roof over their head or food on their table.

Accountability & Policies

501(c)(3) Tax-Exempt Status

The Potter Family Foundation is a recognized 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization. Your contributions are tax-deductible to the extent allowed by law. We maintain public records of our IRS Form 990 to ensure our donors see exactly how their funds are utilized.

Privacy & Donor Data Policy

We respect your privacy. We do not sell, share, or trade our donors' names or personal information with any other entity, nor send mailings to our donors on behalf of other organizations. This policy applies to all information received by the Potter Family Foundation, both online and offline.
